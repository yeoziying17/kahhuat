About Us
Rooted in Johor,
Strengthened by Global Knowledge

Kah Huat Maju Enterprise has been a trusted name in Kahang, Kluang since the 1980s — over 40 years of hands-on experience. Our journey spans multiple countries including India, Hong Kong, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Australia, and New Zealand, bringing world-class knowledge back to Malaysian plantations. We are also strengthened by a valued collaboration with Thunderbird Company, Australia.

What We Do
Our Core Services

From fresh oil palm fruit bunches to electrical fencing and goods transportation — we have the expertise to deliver.

Fresh Oil Palm Fruit Bunches

Production and sale of high-quality fresh fruit bunches (FFB) from well-managed oil palm estates in the Kluang region.

General Construction & Renovation

Reliable construction and renovation works for agricultural facilities, estate buildings, and related infrastructure projects.

Replanting Services

Full-cycle palm oil replanting — from clearing and soil preparation to planting and early maintenance to maximise yield.

Electrical Fencing Installation

Professional installation of electrical fencing systems for estate and plantation security — keeping crops and property protected.

Goods Transportation

Dependable transportation of goods by all means. As experienced transport operators, we ensure timely and safe delivery.
